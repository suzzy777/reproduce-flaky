I think we do need to raise the time limit. The reason there's a timeout might be because we want to fail the tests that are unreasonably slow. But when tests are run in parallel, a build being 5X, or even 20X slower than another is not unreasonable, and we don't rely on these timeouts to catch performance regressions. I think we may just set the timeout to 10 minutes.
